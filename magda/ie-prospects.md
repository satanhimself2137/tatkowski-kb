Dear [HR Team / First Name],

I'm reaching out from Tatkowski Interpreting & Recruitment, an Irish-registered
interpreting and translation agency working with employers across Ireland.

We specialise in supporting companies with multilingual workforces — covering
Polish, Ukrainian, Russian, Romanian, Portuguese and a range of other languages.

For [sector-specific descriptor, e.g. "dairy processing businesses like Dairygold"], we typically help with:

• Employment contracts and offer letters translated for non-English speaking staff
• Health & safety materials, induction packs and training documents
• On-site interpreter support for HR meetings, disciplinary processes
or return to work interviews
• Any other employee communications that need to be clearly understood
across language barriers

We work on a flexible, per-request basis with no long-term commitment required —
so it's easy to try the service when the need arises.

Would you be open to a brief call or email exchange to explore whether we could
support your team?

Kind Regards,
