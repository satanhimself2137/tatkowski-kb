# Team Log — Ideas

Append-only park of non-trivial ideas surfaced in TEAM ONE WhatsApp or Claude conversations — things worth revisiting but not yet acted on. Written automatically by the watcher when an idea meets the bar; can also be written manually from desktop Claude via `kb.ps1`.

**Format:** each entry is a block, newest on top. Watcher Claude prepends new entries — never edits existing ones. Corrections happen via desktop Claude.

**What counts as an idea:**
- A concrete suggestion with a potential next step ("offer offshore-rig interpreters a bundle with annual contract translation")
- An observation that could become a workstream ("Algarve real estate agents handling Portuguese-to-English certs is an underserved niche")
- A workflow improvement worth trying ("auto-tag B2B clients in SalesManager by NAICS code")

**What does NOT count:**
- Idle "wouldn't it be cool if…" with no specifics
- Restating known plans or active workstreams
- Things already in a roadmap file
- Decisions (those go in `decisions.md`)

---

## Entry format

```
### YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC — <one-line idea headline>

**Surfaced by:** <Maciej | David | Magda | Team>
**Source:** <TEAM ONE WhatsApp | direct chat URL>
**Detail:** <2–4 lines, Claude's distillation of the idea and why it might matter. Not a verbatim paste.>
**Possible next step:** <one line, optional — only if obvious. Otherwise leave blank.>
```
